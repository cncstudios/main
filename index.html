New Group, Who This?

Hello there! We are the current C&C Studios Team and we would like to give you a warm welcome to our website! Within C&C Studios we focus, create, and consult primarily in three digital art mediums: photopgrahy, editing, and website development.

To know us a bit, our organisation was founded in Southern California in 2018, but we are not tied to any location! We are always open to add more people into C&C Studios as long as you keep an open mind and yearn to learn more about the digital arts! All of our members are knowledgeable in at least one of the listed programs (from the icons) and are constantly learning more!

As of right now, our organisation-wide code-project is Bobaddict: a mobile application in which helps you find the best boba tea in all of Southern California! The application is currently in the design and prototype stage, but development has never stopped. The project is being built upon a current $0 budget as we work on it in our pasttime, so feel free to ask if you can join the Development Team!

Monthly Edits

As a part of continous learning, we upload our digital edits to our Google Drive for other members to view and be inspired to create unique works of art! This part of C&C Studios focuses only on photography and/or the editing of them. Categorising each member's works by month, we select our own favourite work to be displayed for you to see! If you are intrigued by them, you can probably find similar works on our own respective social media accounts.
